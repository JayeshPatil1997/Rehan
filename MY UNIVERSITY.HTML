Meassage From the Cheancellor
A University is always a place of light, liberty, and learning.
This dictum still inspires me and I believe, it takes team-work to build successful institutions.
I welcome you to IES University to join hands in laying the foundation of your future with values, wisdom, and knowledge.

Er. B.S. Yadav, Chancellor IES University is an Engineer and Founder of the InfoTech Education Society which runs the IES University. He completed his Bachelor of Engineering in 1994 from Govt. Engineering College (now UIT RGPV, Bhopal). With a vision and commitment towards Quality Education and for preparing the best human resources as per the acceptable global standards, he conceptualized and founded the InfoTech Education Society in 1999, which began serving the society at large through IES College of Education, Bhopal.
Now, IES University imparts education in the disciplines of Engineering & Technology, Management, Education, Pharmaceutical Sciences, Agricultural Sciences, Nursing & Para-Medical Courses, Fashion Designing, Teacher’s Education, Research i.e., from Nursery Schooling to Ph. D. level. In view of the increasing global demand for skilled manpower, Polytechnic & Diploma courses are also taught at IES campus.
Care is taken to acquaint the students with the changing global trends & scenarios so as to make them world citizens with versatile & multifarious skills, so as to be liked, accepted, and appreciated in the society.
Chancellor, Er. B. S. Yadav has led efforts to Provide Professional Education to meritorious students who are ambitious in terms of their aspirations, hailing from moderate income background and are looking for quality education at affordable cost, among first-generation students at IES University and its other Colleges.
His efforts have attracted national attention and from renowned quarters like Mr. Anand Kumar, Founder of “Super 30”, Patna. He has emphasized the importance of both the value of research and inclusivity to IES’s mission of being a world class University with state of the art infrastructure for the benefits of students and has championed the University’s commitment to be the breeding ground for the top notch professionals and has launched initiatives designed to fortify IES University’s connections with the Industry in Central India and beyond. He has also been a leading voice in University’s unification to the Innovation ecosystem through programs like the Incubation Cell, etc. leading to the inculcation of entrepreneurial bent of mind among the students.
Under the able guidance of its Founder Chancellor, IES University has been awarded MP’s First Green Campus and IES Group Engineering College has now been ranked amongst the Top Engineering Institutes of India by leading media houses like India Today (3rd Rank), Career 360, The Week, Dataquest, The Outlook, etc.
His leadership and professional skills, his contribution in the field of education have won him the honor of being the President of RGPV, Alumni Association; as Member, he was on the Advisory Board of the LIC of India, Central Zone and Jury Member of the FMPCCI Award Committee along with membership of several Technical organizations and Professional bodies including NASSCOM, Institute of Engineers (IE) apart from being the Co-chairperson of CII and M.P. State Education & Skill Panel, among others.
He has Participated in Indian Higher Education Delegation to the UK for consulting & meeting with Cambridge, Oxford and lvy League Institutions regarding students exchange programs and also participated in capacity building workshop on Higher Education conducted at IIM, Indore.
